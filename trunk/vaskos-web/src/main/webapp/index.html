VASKOS validator!

This is a proof of concept to implement the SKOS integrity constraints based on:

  • ShEX, or Shape Expressions(intro), to express constraints and shape of SKOS-based resources.
  • SPARQL queries, to check more detailed integrity constraints in SKOS-based resources.
